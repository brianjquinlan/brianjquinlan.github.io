What is Convr Intake?

The intake product has two main purposes. First is ownership over the API that controls the submission process an underwriter will go through when quoting a business. Basically this is the e2e process a user takes on the platform. The second is the automating the ingestion of documents. Think things like insurance related pdfs/word docs/excels/emails.
